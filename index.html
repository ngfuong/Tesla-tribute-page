Nikola Tesla

Key life events:

  • 1859: Born in Smilijan, Siberia.
  • 1875: Enrolled in a Polytechnic in Siberia.
  • 1880: Moved to Prague to study philosophy.
  • 1881: Moved to Budapest to work in Budapest Telephone Exchange company as chief electrical engineer.
  • 1882: Worked for Thomas Edison at Continental Edison Company in Paris.
  • 1885: Relocated to the USA and began submitting patents.
  • 1887: Re-imagined the Electric Motor.
  • 1890: The 'Tesla coil'.
  • 1943: Died in a hotel room in New York.
